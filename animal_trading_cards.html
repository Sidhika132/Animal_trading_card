Javan_tiger

They had relatively long nose and which led scientists to believe that Javan tiger was a separate species Panthera sondaica.

  • Scientific NamePanthera tigris sondaica
  • Average Length200 to 245 cm
  • Average Lifespan20 years
  • HabitatNatural Habitat

The Javan tiger was a Panthera tigris sondaica population, which lived in the Indonesian island of Java until the mid 1970s. It was hunted to extinction and its natural habitat converted for human use. It was one of the three tiger populations in the Sunda Islands.
